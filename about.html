Our Story

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

For over 10 years, we've been providing personalized, dignified in-home care for seniors and special needs individuals — built on the simple belief that everyone deserves to be treated like family.

How It All Began

Founded on Family Values

LilikoiCare was born from a lifetime of purpose-driven work. With about 25 years of experience working with vulnerable populations on social and emotional issues across the lifespan, our founders understood deeply what it means to provide care that truly makes a difference.

That experience shaped everything we do today. Recognizing that seniors and individuals with special needs thrive best in the comfort and familiarity of their own homes, we built LilikoiCare to deliver personalized, dignified in-home support — rooted in decades of hands-on expertise.

Our Mission

Care for Others as You Would Have Them Care for You

Our mission is grounded in the golden rule. Every decision we make, every caregiver we hire, and every care plan we create starts with one question: would this be good enough for our own family?

We believe that living at home should be safe, comfortable, and dignified — whether you're aging gracefully or navigating life with a disability. Our commitment is to provide the highest standard of non-medical home care, enabling seniors and special needs individuals to maintain their independence while receiving the support they need.
