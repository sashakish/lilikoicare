Caregiving Careers

More Than a Job — It's a Calling

At Lilikoi Care, we seek compassionate individuals for non-medical, in-home care roles supporting seniors and special needs individuals. Our caregivers become important figures in the lives of those they serve, building meaningful relationships that go far beyond task completion.

As a privately-owned local company, our team functions like a family — without corporate pressure, with genuine support, and with the flexibility you need to maintain a healthy work-life balance.

Why Join Us

Benefits of Working with Lilikoi Care

We believe in taking care of the people who take care of others. Here's what sets us apart as an employer.

Flexible Scheduling

Days, evenings, nights, weekends, and live-in positions available. We customize arrangements based on your needs and life circumstances.

One-on-One Care Model

Serve individual clients exclusively, enabling focused attention and meaningful relationships rather than managing multiple assignments.

Personal Satisfaction

Become an important figure in a senior's life during their golden years. The relationships you build are deeply rewarding and meaningful.

Family Culture

A privately-owned local company where team members are treated as family. No corporate pressures — just genuine care and support.

24/7 Support

Live staff available around the clock by phone. We listen to your scheduling needs and help accommodate changes as life evolves.

Training & Development

Complete our orientation program and earn professional certification through our e-learning courses. Invest in your career growth with us.

The Role

What You'll Do

As a caregiver with Lilikoi Care, you'll provide non-medical in-home support to seniors, helping them maintain their independence and quality of life.

  • Companionship and meaningful conversation
  • Meal preparation and planning
  • Light housekeeping and home organization
  • General assistance with daily activities
  • Personal care assistance including bathing, dressing, and grooming
  • Running simple errands and appointments
  • Engaging clients in fun, stimulating activities
  • Medication reminders and health monitoring
